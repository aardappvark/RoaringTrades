Roaring Trades

A 1920s Prohibition-era trading game for Solana Seeker

About

Start with $1,500 and 30 days to build your bootlegging empire across six Chicago neighborhoods. Buy low, sell high, dodge the cops, and climb the leaderboard.

Features

  • 5 tradeable goods with dynamic pricing
  • 6 Chicago neighborhoods with unique risk/reward
  • 7 upgradeable vehicles from bicycle to zeppelin
  • Police chases, gang encounters, and heat mechanics
  • Speakeasy investments for passive income
  • 17 claimable achievements
  • Solana wallet leaderboard integration
  • No analytics, no tracking - privacy first
